\section{Harmonic Analysis}

\subsection{Objective of the Harmonic Analysis}

Harmonic analysis makes it possible to study the steady-state response of the system to a sinusoidal excitation with variable frequency. This analysis is used to identify resonances, dynamic amplifications and critical areas of displacement or stress.

The harmonic analysis must be defined based on the results obtained in the modal analysis. In particular, the frequency range and the direction of excitation must be related to the natural frequencies and mode shapes identified previously.
