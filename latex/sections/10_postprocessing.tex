\subsection{Quality of the Graphs}

The graphs included in this report meet the following minimum requirements:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Explanatory title or descriptive figure caption.
    \item Axis labels.
    \item Units indicated.
    \item Legend when several curves are shown.
    \item Appropriate scale.
    \item Identification of the most important resonance peaks.
\end{itemize}

When different cases are compared (e.g.\ the damping values $\zeta = 1\%$, $\zeta = 5\%$ and $\zeta = 10\%$), the curves are plotted on the same scale so that the comparison is clear.
